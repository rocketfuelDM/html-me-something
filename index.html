Normandy, MO

"This was the first placed I lived. It was a really cool place to live at first. The whole community came together once per year to celebrate. We had a neighborhood parade and bbq every year. All of us kids would run around all night and the parents socialized...it brings back fond memories. I lived here until I turned 13.

Creve Coeur, MO

"Located in West St. Louis County, Creve Coeur is best known for it's great lake; Creve Coeur Lake. I lived here until 2000"

O'Fallon, MO

"I moved to O'Fallon, Missouri after highschool. At the time I had very little experience with St. Charles County. I have now lived here for 17 years and couldn't be any more pleased. O'Fallon is a great place to raise a family."
